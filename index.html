p5.sound interactive guide  ·  Topic 13

▶ Playback Control

.play() plays once · .loop() plays continuously · .pause() holds position · .stop() rewinds · .isPlaying() returns true/false

⏹ Stopped

🎚 Volume & Playback Rate

setVolume(0–1) controls amplitude. .rate() changes speed & pitch — 1.0 is normal, >1.0 speeds up (higher pitch), <1.0 slows down (lower pitch).

⌨ Keyboard Soundboard

keyPressed() checks the key variable to trigger sounds. mousePressed() fires a one-shot play on canvas click.

P — Play / Pause
S — Stop
🖱 Canvas click — Play

🔊 Amplitude Visualizer

An AnalyserNode measures sound level each frame (equivalent to p5.Amplitude + amp.getLevel()). The circle and bars react to the music in real time.
